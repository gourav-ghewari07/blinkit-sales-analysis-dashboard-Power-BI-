🛒 Blinkit Sales Analysis Dashboard

📌 Project Overview

The Blinkit Sales Analysis Dashboard is an interactive Power BI project designed to analyze sales performance, customer engagement, delivery efficiency, and marketing campaign effectiveness.

The dashboard transforms raw business data into actionable insights that help stakeholders monitor key performance indicators (KPIs), identify trends, and make data-driven decisions.

---

🎯 Business Objective

The primary objectives of this project are:

- Monitor sales and order performance.
- Analyze customer engagement across marketing channels.
- Evaluate delivery performance and efficiency.
- Identify high-performing campaigns.
- Support business growth through data-driven insights.

📈 Dashboard Features

Executive Summary

Provides a quick overview of:

- Total Orders
- Total Sales
- Customer Ratings
- Delivery Ratings
- Delivery Distance

Marketing Channel Analysis

Analyzes audience engagement across:

- App
- Email
- Social Media
- SMS

Audience segments include:

- All Users
- Active Users
- New Users
- Premium Users

Campaign Performance Analysis

Measures campaign effectiveness for:

- Referral Programs
- New User Campaigns
- Email Campaigns
- Category Promotions
- Weekend Campaigns
- Flash Sales
- App Push Notifications
- Membership Programs
- Festival Offers

Delivery Status Analysis

Tracks order delivery performance:

- On Time
- Slightly Delayed
- Significantly Delayed

Year-wise Order Analysis

Compares order distribution across multiple years to evaluate business growth trends.

Customer Engagement Trends

Visualizes impressions, conversions, and engagement patterns over time.

🔍 Key Insights

1. Strong Sales Performance

The platform generated over 1.20 million in sales through 5000 completed orders, indicating healthy business activity.

2. App Channel Generated High Engagement

The application channel consistently attracted high audience engagement compared to other marketing channels.

3. Referral Programs Performed Best

Referral-based campaigns generated the highest engagement and conversion values among all campaigns.

4. Delivery Performance Remains Strong

Most deliveries were completed on time, contributing positively to customer satisfaction.

5. Customer Ratings Indicate Improvement Opportunities

While overall ratings remain positive, there is potential to improve customer experience and increase satisfaction levels.

6. Customer Activity Shows Periodic Peaks

Several periods recorded significantly higher engagement, indicating the effectiveness of promotional activities and seasonal demand.
